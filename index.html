The History of .btc Domains

From early concepts to the Ordinals revolution

Early Concepts

Over a decade ago, the Bitcoin community proposed domain solutions but faced technical limitations.

Fragmented Ecosystem

Many projects use .btc without rights because they are not on-chain on Bitcoin and do not serve the true purpose.

Ordinals Revolution

In 2023, Ordinals enabled true decentralized .btc domains on Bitcoin's mainnet.

Community Adoption

Early inscriptions like "godoftunder.btc" (#18681) sparked massive community adoption.

First 6D Minted Out

.btc became the first decentralized identity system on Bitcoin, with 6D domains minted out.

Record Growth

Nearly 2 million domains registered, surpassing ENS and becoming the most used blockchain naming system.
